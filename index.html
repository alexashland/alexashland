Bio

PhD candidate at the University of Iowa. Studying 19th and 20th century American literature. Research Assistant in the Walt Whitman Archive.

Interests

Media Studies, Literary Theory, Poetry and Poetics, Digital Humanities.

Quotes

But as in landlessness alone resides the highest truth, shoreless, indefinite as God—so, better is it to perish in that howling infinite"—Melville

Influences

Whitman, Melville, Reznikoff, Morrison, Dickinson, Emerson, Stowe, Hemingway, Du Bois, Oliver, Stevens, Pound, O'Brien, Borges.
